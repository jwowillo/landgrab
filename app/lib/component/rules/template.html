Landgrab is a game where each player tries to destroy all the other player's pieces. The rules are:

  • Players have {{ rules?.pieceCount }} pieces on an {{ rules?.boardSize }} by {{ rules?.boardSize }} board represented as a grid.
  • Pieces have {{ rules?.life }} life and do {{ rules?.damage }} damage initially.
  • Player can optionally move any of their pieces one cell in the 8 cardinal directions.
  • Collisions between pieces cause the current player's pieces to damage all contacting enemy units equal to their damage amounts.
  • Pieces that have taken damage greater than or equal to their life are removed from the board.
  • Pieces gain a level when they participate in destroying another piece, increasing their damage by {{ rules?.damageIncrease }} and life by {{ rules?.lifeIncrease }}.
  • Players with no pieces at the end of a turn lose.
  • Players have {{ rules?.timerDuration?.inSeconds }} seconds to move each turn.
