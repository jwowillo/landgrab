landgrab is a board game where each player tries to destroy all of the other player's pieces The rules are:

  • Each player has n = 5 pieces on a 2n + 1 by 2n + 1 board represented as a grid.
  • Each piece has 3 life and does 1 damage initially.
  • Each player can optionally move any of their pieces one space in the 8 cardinal directions on the board per turn.
  • Collisions between pieces cause the current player's pieces to damage all contacting enemy units equal to their damage attributes.
  • Pieces are removed from the board, or destroyed, if they have taken damage greater than or equal to their life.
  • Pieces gain a level when they participate in destroying another piece, allowing their damage and life to be increased by 1.
  • Players with no pieces at the end of a turn lose.
  • Each player has 30 seconds to make a move each turn.
